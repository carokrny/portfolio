Hi! I'm Carolyn and I'm learning to code. I graduated summa cum laude from Penn in 2018 with a degree in Materials Science & Engineering. I then passed the patent bar and practiced patent law for two years.

In January 2020 I was in an accident, later had brain surgery, and I've been slowly recovering ever since. I'm now visually impaired. I can't see depth, motion, or read very well, which makes it hard to practice patent law. So I'm using adaptive technologies to learn to code!

I started learning fullstack development in July 2021. Computer science isn't totally new to me, I took two years of object-oriented programming in high school and some computer science classes in college. It feels good to get back into it.

For a long time after the brain injury I felt stuck inside my own head, unable to do any of my old hobbies. Now I can build, create, and problem solve, and it feels so so good.
